Support FAQ

1. Support Channels
Customers can contact customer support via email or live chat.
Support contact details are available on the company website.

2. Support Hours
Customer support is available from 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM, Monday to Saturday.
Support is not available on public holidays.

3. Response Time
Emails are usually responded to within 24 hours.
Live chat responses are typically immediate during working hours.

4. Issue Escalation
If an issue is not resolved within 48 hours, it can be escalated to the support manager.
Escalations should include the ticket number and issue details.

5. Common Issues
Customers can contact support for issues related to orders, refunds, payments, and account access.
Technical issues are handled by the technical support team.
