Refund Policy

1. Refund Eligibility
Customers are eligible to request a refund within 7 days of purchase.
Refund requests made after 7 days will not be accepted.

2. Conditions for Refund
The product must be unused and in its original condition.
Proof of purchase is required for all refund requests.

3. Non-Refundable Items
Discounted products are non-refundable.
Promotional or clearance items are not eligible for refunds.

4. Refund Process
Once a refund request is approved, it is processed within 5 to 7 business days.
Refunds are credited back to the original mode of payment.

5. Failed or Delayed Refunds
If a refund is not received within 7 business days, customers should contact support.
