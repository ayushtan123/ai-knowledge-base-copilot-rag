Leave Policy

1. Total Leave Entitlement
Employees are entitled to 24 paid leaves per year.

2. Types of Leave
The total of 24 paid leaves includes:
- 12 Casual Leaves
- 12 Sick Leaves

3. Casual Leave
Casual leave can be used for personal matters or short-term needs.
Casual leave should be applied at least one day in advance.
A maximum of two casual leaves can be taken consecutively.

4. Sick Leave
Sick leave can be used in case of illness or medical emergencies.
Sick leave exceeding two consecutive days requires a medical certificate.
Employees should inform their manager as early as possible in case of sickness.

5. Leave Application Process
All leaves must be applied through the HR portal.

6. Carry Forward and Lapse
Unused leaves cannot be carried forward to the next year.
All unused leaves lapse at the end of the calendar year.
