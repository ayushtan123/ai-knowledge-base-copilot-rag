Employee Handbook

1. Introduction
This handbook outlines the general policies, rules, and expectations for all employees.
All employees are expected to read and follow the guidelines mentioned in this document.

2. Working Hours
Regular working hours are from 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM, Monday to Friday.
Employees are expected to be available during core working hours from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM.
Saturday and Sunday are weekly offs unless otherwise specified by the management.

3. Attendance Policy
Employees must mark their attendance daily using the official attendance system.
Late arrivals or early departures may be monitored and reviewed periodically.
Frequent attendance issues may result in disciplinary action.

4. Code of Conduct
Employees must maintain professional behavior at all times.
Harassment, discrimination, or inappropriate conduct will not be tolerated.

5. Leave Overview
Employees are entitled to paid leaves as per the Leave Policy.
All leave requests must be approved by the reporting manager.

6. Confidentiality
Employees must not share confidential company or customer information.
Violation of confidentiality rules may lead to termination.
